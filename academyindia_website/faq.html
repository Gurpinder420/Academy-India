Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What courses does AcademyIndia offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of courses including Web Development (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP & MySQL), UI/UX Design, Data Analytics, AI & Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing with AWS, Python Programming, Java & Spring Boot, C/C++, and career skills courses like Interview Preparation and Typing.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes! We provide 100% placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, mock interviews, soft skills training, and direct referrals to our 200+ partner companies. Our dedicated placement cell works tirelessly to help you land your dream job. We have successfully placed over 3,500 students at top companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, and more.

What are the batch timings?

We offer flexible batch timings to suit everyone's schedule: Morning batches (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM), Afternoon batches (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM), Evening batches (5:00 PM - 8:00 PM), and Weekend batches (Saturday & Sunday). We also provide fast-track options for working professionals who want to complete courses in a shorter timeframe.

Are there any prerequisites for joining?

Most of our courses are designed for beginners with no prior experience required. For advanced courses like AI/ML and Data Science, basic programming knowledge is recommended but not mandatory. Our instructors will guide you from the basics. For office courses like MS Office and Tally, only basic computer knowledge is needed.

Do you provide certificates after course completion?

Yes, we provide industry-recognized certificates upon successful completion of each course. Additionally, we help students prepare for and obtain international certifications from Google, Microsoft, AWS, CompTIA, Python Institute, and Oracle. These certifications significantly enhance your resume and career prospects.

Can I get a demo class before enrolling?

Absolutely! We offer free demo classes for all our courses. You can attend a session, interact with our instructors, and experience our teaching methodology before making a decision. Simply fill out the enquiry form or call us to schedule your free demo class.

What is the class size?

We maintain small batch sizes of 8-12 students to ensure personalized attention and better learning outcomes. This allows our instructors to focus on each student's progress and provide individual guidance when needed.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help. Reach out to us for personalized assistance.
