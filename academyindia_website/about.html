About AcademyIndia

Empowering students with future-ready tech skills since 2014

Building Tech Leaders of Tomorrow

AcademyIndia Computer Institute was founded in 2014 with a vision to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. Located in the heart of Mumbai at Jogeshwari West, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in computer education.

Our institute offers a comprehensive range of courses from basic computer literacy to advanced programming, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. We believe in practical, hands-on learning that prepares students for real-world challenges.

With a team of experienced instructors, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and strong industry connections, we have helped over 5,000 students launch successful careers in technology.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality technology education that empowers individuals to build successful careers in the digital economy. We strive to make every student industry-ready through practical training and personalized mentorship.

Our Vision

To become India's leading computer training institute, recognized for producing skilled tech professionals who drive innovation and digital transformation across industries worldwide.

Our Values

Excellence in education, integrity in operations, innovation in teaching methods, inclusivity in access, and commitment to student success define everything we do at AcademyIndia.
