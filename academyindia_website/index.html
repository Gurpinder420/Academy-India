Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What courses does AcademyIndia offer?

We offer a wide range of courses including MS Office, Advanced Excel, Tally Prime, Web Development (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP & MySQL, WordPress), Graphic Designing (Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW, Premiere Pro, Figma), UI/UX Design, Digital Marketing (SEO, Google Analytics, Social Media Marketing), Data Analytics (Power BI, Tableau), AI & Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, and more.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes! We provide 100% placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, mock interviews, and direct referrals to our 200+ partner companies. Our dedicated placement cell works tirelessly to help you land your dream job.

What are the batch timings?

We offer flexible batch timings to suit everyone's schedule: Morning batches (9 AM - 12 PM), Afternoon batches (1 PM - 4 PM), Evening batches (5 PM - 8 PM), and Weekend batches (Saturday & Sunday). We also provide fast-track options for working professionals.

Are there any prerequisites for joining?

Most of our courses are designed for beginners with no prior experience required. For advanced courses like AI/ML and Data Science, basic programming knowledge is recommended but not mandatory. Our instructors will guide you from the basics.
